About us

OUR PHILOSOPHY

Step Dezign is a multidisciplinary firm, focused on interior design, lighting design and planning.

We are a team of professional, energetic individual’s with talented designers and experienced managers available to guide our client’s through the flawless and timely execution of any residential design project.

We manipulate forms and combinations from our everyday world to create an aesthetic, practical, and sustainable atmosphere that transforms a simple building or space into a place of happiness for you and your guests.

OUR STORY

Step Dezign PTE LTD was established in 2019 after assembling works over the years with designers and commercial builders in Singapore. We are here to provide our expertise to those who are in need of design sense.

Design consult is the basis of what we do, with flexibility to move beyond.

We believe a successful interior combines both form and function and is in alignment with the client’s aesthetic, lifestyle and budget. It is therefore important each of our designers has the right kind of experience.
