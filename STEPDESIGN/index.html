About Us

Step Dezign PTE LTD was established in 2019 after assembling works over the years with designers and commercial builders in Singapore. We are here to provide our expertise to those who are in need of design sense.

Design consult is the basis of what we do, with flexibility to move beyond.

We believe a successful interior combines both form and function and is in alignment with the client’s aesthetic, lifestyle and budget. It is therefore important each of our designers has the right kind of experience.
