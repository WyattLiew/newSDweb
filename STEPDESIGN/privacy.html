Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. The purpose of this Privacy Policy is to inform you of how STEP DEZIGN PTE LTD collects and manages personal data in accordance with the Personal Data Protection Act 2012 of Singapore ("DPA").

By interacting with us, submitting information to us or signing up for any promotions or services offered by us, you agree and consent for STEP DEZIGN PTE LTD and authorised service providers and third parties to collect, use and disclose and/or retain your personal data in the manner set forth in this Privacy Policy.

This Privacy Policy supplements but does not supersede nor replace any other consent which you may have previously provided to us nor does it affect any right that we may have at law in connection with the collection, use, disclosure and/or retention of your personal data.

1. Your Personal Data

Generally, we may collect your Personal Data through the following ways:

  • when you sign up for an account via the website or mobile app;
  • when you submit an enquiry or a quote request with us;
  • when you sign up for events;
  • when you sign up or redeem a promotional offer or initiatives;
  • when you ask to be included in an email or other mailing list;
  • when you request that we contact you and/or interacted with our customer servicing team via our communication channels such as a phone call which will be recorded;
  • when you choose to opt in and/or engage in services that requires validation of Personal Data such as submitting a review;
  • when you submit a job application;
  • when you perform an online transaction;
  • when you respond to our request for additional Personal Data;
  • when you submit your Personal Data to us for any other reason.

If you provide us with any Personal Data relating to a third party (e.g. information of spouse, children, parents, employees and/or authorised representatives), by submitting such information to us, you represent to us that you have obtained the consent of the third party to you providing us with their Personal Data for the respective purposes.

You should ensure that all Personal Data submitted to us is complete, accurate, true and correct. Failure on your part to do so may result in our inability to provide you with products and services you have requested.

3. Purposes for the Collection, Use and Disclosure of Your Personal Data

Generally, STEP DEZIGN PTE LTD collect, use, disclose and/or retain your Personal Data for the following purposes:

  • to respond to your queries, requests and complaints;
  • to verify your identity for the use of our goods, services (example: reviews) and transactions;
  • to keep you updated on our events;
  • to provide you with the goods and services which you have signed up for, including on Promotions;
  • to push articles to you which may be relevant to you;

  • to conduct market research for statistical, profiling and statistical analysis for the improvement of services provided to you;
  • to prevent, detect and investigate criminal acts such as money laundering and fraud, and managing other business risks;
  • to comply with any applicable rules, laws and regulations, codes of practice or guidelines or to assist in law enforcement and investigations by relevant authorities.

As part of your registration on STEP DEZIGN PTE LTD, you agree that you may receive certain administrative communications from STEP DEZIGN PTE LTD, and you will not be able to opt out of receiving such administrative communications.

STEP DEZIGN PTE LTD will take reasonable steps to protect your Personal Data against unauthorised disclosure. We maintain appropriate security safeguards and practices to protect your Personal Data unauthorised access, collection, use, disclosure, copying, modification, disposal or similar risks, in accordance with applicable laws.
